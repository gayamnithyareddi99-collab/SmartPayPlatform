About SmartPayPlatform

How SmartPayPlatform Works

SmartPayPlatform transforms traditional fraud detection into a real-time, AI-driven decision system that prevents fraud before transactions are completed.

01

System Integration

SmartPayPlatform connects securely with payment gateways, PSPs, and financial systems via APIs, enabling real-time access to transaction data across multiple payment channels.

02

Real-Time Transaction Analysis

Every transaction is analyzed instantly using machine learning models that evaluate behavioural patterns, device data, and historical activity to identify anomalies.

03

Predictive Fraud Decisioning

Each transaction is assigned a fraud risk score before authorisation, enabling businesses to approve, block, or trigger additional verification in real time.

04

Automation & Compliance Reporting

Fraud workflows are automated and all actions are logged into audit-ready reports aligned with FCA, PSD2, and GDPR, ensuring compliance and operational transparency.

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn how SmartPayPlatform helps fintech companies and digital businesses detect fraud in real time, reduce risk, and optimise payment performance.

What is SmartPayPlatform?

SmartPayPlatform is an AI-driven fraud detection and payment optimisation platform designed for fintech companies and high-volume digital businesses. It provides real-time transaction monitoring and predictive fraud scoring before payments are authorised.

What problem does the platform solve?

Many businesses rely on static or rule-based fraud systems that detect fraud only after transactions occur. SmartPayPlatform identifies risk before authorisation, helping prevent financial loss and reduce chargebacks.

How does fraud detection work?

The platform uses machine learning models to analyse transaction behaviour, device data, and historical patterns in real time. Each transaction is assigned a risk score, allowing businesses to approve, block, or verify payments instantly.

Who is SmartPayPlatform designed for?

SmartPayPlatform is built for fintech companies, payment service providers, online platforms, and businesses handling high volumes of digital transactions.

Does the platform support compliance?

Yes. SmartPayPlatform provides automated compliance reporting aligned with regulatory frameworks such as FCA, PSD2, and GDPR, ensuring audit-ready records.

Can SmartPayPlatform scale across multiple systems?

Yes. The platform integrates with multiple payment providers and APIs, allowing businesses to monitor transactions across different systems in one unified dashboard.
